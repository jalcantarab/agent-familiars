# Code Of Conduct

Be direct, generous, and practical.

This project is about small desktop companions, but the usual open-source rules
still apply:

- Treat people with respect.
- Critique the work, not the person.
- Do not submit hateful, harassing, sexualized, or violent pet concepts.
- Do not submit copyrighted characters, company mascots, or assets you cannot
  license for redistribution.
- Keep issues and pull requests focused enough that someone can actually review
  them.

Maintainers may close, edit, or remove contributions that make the project less
safe, less useful, or less legally clear. That is not drama. That is gardening.
