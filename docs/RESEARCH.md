# Research Notes

Research date: 2026-05-15.

## Product And Audience Read

Codex pets are a good fit for people who spend long stretches with agentic
coding tools open: software engineers, infra teams, open-source maintainers,
AI-native founders, technical PMs, design engineers, and people coordinating
reviews or CI fixes with assistants.

The strongest pets for this audience are not generic cute mascots. They map to
real work rhythms:

- terminal work and shell fluency
- PR review and diff inspection
- debugging, tests, and CI failures
- waiting for human approval
- token limits and context management
- product planning and agent delegation

## Licensing Filter

For this public repository, import only:

- original assets we created
- MIT or Apache/BSD-style assets with attribution
- CC BY assets with attribution
- third-party submissions with explicit redistribution permission

Do not import by default:

- non-commercial assets
- copyrighted anime/game/movie characters
- brand mascots or logos we do not own
- personal pets or portraits unless the creator clearly licensed them for reuse
- assets with no license
