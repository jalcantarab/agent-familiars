# First 50 Pet Roadmap

This is the first public curation target: 50 pets that feel native to people
using coding agents every day.

## Motion Standards

Every entry below has a motion DNA record in
`catalog/first-50-motion.json`. The top ten launch briefs are expanded in
`catalog/flagship-10.json`, and the repo-wide rules are in
`docs/ANIMATION_BIBLE.md`.

The flagship ten are not just the first ten installed pets. They are the first

## External Candidates To Evaluate

These are worth tracking but not importing until licensing and redistribution
rights are clean:

- CC BY-NC galleries with original submissions: ask creators for commercial redistribution permission.
- Fan-art packs: link as external inspiration only, unless the upstream IP owner allows redistribution.
- No-license personal pet repos: ask for explicit license before copying assets.
- Tooling repos: integrate as ecosystem links instead of pet imports.
